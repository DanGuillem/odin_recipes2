Chilli Prawn Pasta Recipe

Description

This recipe combines prawns, linquine, chilli and parmesan

Ingredients

  • Prawns 200g
  • linquine 500g
  • One red chilli, finely diced
  • One Garlic Bulb, minced
  • One Shallot, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp Butter unsalted
  • 150ml White Wine
  • Parmesan Cheese grated
  • 250g Cherry Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Add butter to hot pan and fry prawns until pink, then remove to seperate plate
  2. Meanwhile, boil a pot of salted water and add linquine to the pot
  3. To the same pan where the prawns cooked, add shallots and chilli and cook until soft and fragrant
  4. Add white wine to the pan and reduce to simmer, add tomatoes and cook until halfway reduced
  5. Once pasta has finished cooking, add to the pan with a splash of pasta water
  6. Cook for another two minutes and then plate, garnishing with Parmesan
